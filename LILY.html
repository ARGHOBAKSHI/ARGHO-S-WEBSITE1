ABOUT LILY

The lily flower is beautiful and aromatic in appearance.They are erect perennial plants wiith leafy stems,scaly bulbs,usually narrow leaves and solitary or clustered flowers.The flowers consist of six petal-like segments,which may form the shape of a trumpet,with more or less elongated tube,as in Madonna lily and Easter lily It is noted for its beauty,aroma and shape.Its flower pedals have brownish or pinkish lines on the outside.
