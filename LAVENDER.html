ABOUT LAVENDER

Lavender is a perennial plant that can live upto 20 years if the conditions are optimum.It produces purple flowers,which contain high levels of essential oil.The essintial oil of lavender is recognised globally as a respected commodity.The oil of lavender is used in some medicines.In ancient Egypt,lavender was used during mummification process to perfume the mummies(Preserved bodies of dead people).
